Frequently Asked Questions

Is the converter really free?

+

Yes! Our Word to PDF converter is 100% free. You can convert as many documents as you want without paying anything. There are no hidden charges or subscriptions required.

What file formats are supported?

+

We support DOC, DOCX, RTF, and other common Word formats. Files can be up to 50MB in size. If you need to convert other formats, please contact us for information about future support.

Is my file secure during conversion?

+

Absolutely! All file transfers are encrypted using SSL technology. Your files are processed on our secure servers and are automatically deleted after conversion. We never store your files or share them with third parties.

Do I need to install any software?

+

No! Our converter is completely online. You don't need to install any software on your computer. Just visit our website, upload your file, and download the converted PDF. It works with any modern web browser.

Will the formatting be preserved?

+

Yes, our advanced conversion engine preserves all formatting, fonts, colors, images, and layout from your original Word document. Your PDF will look exactly like the original document.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

+

Yes! You can upload and convert multiple Word documents at once. Our batch conversion feature allows you to process several files simultaneously, saving you time and effort.

What if my file is larger than 50MB?

+

Currently, our service supports files up to 50MB. If you have larger files, please consider splitting them into smaller parts or contact our support team for alternative solutions.

How long does the conversion take?

+

Most files are converted in just a few seconds. Large files or high server load may take longer. On average, expect conversion to complete within 30 seconds for typical Word documents.
